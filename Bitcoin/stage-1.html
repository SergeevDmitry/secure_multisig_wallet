Step 1/11

Download and install Electrum wallet for your OS.

Step 2/11

Disconnect from internet.

Step 3/11

Run Electrum. Wallet name doesn’t matter on this step. Click “Next”

Step 4/11

Select “Multi-signature wallet” option and click “Next”

Step 5/11

Set number of total cosigners to 4 and number of required signatures to 3. Click “Next”

Step 6/11

Select “Create a new seed” option. Click “Next”

Step 7/11

Select “Standard” seed type option. Click “Next”

Step 8/11

Copy seed in a safe place. Click “Next”

Step 9/11

Confirm seed by typing them in manually. Click “Next”

Step 10/11

Save master public key.

Step 11/11

Close Electrum wallet, connect to internet and send your master public key to cosigners via telegram group.
