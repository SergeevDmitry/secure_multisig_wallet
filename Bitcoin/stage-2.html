Step 1/11

Disconnect from internet.

Step 2/11

Run Electrum, choose a wallet name. Click “Next”

Step 3/11

Repeat steps 4 and 5 from the previous stage. (Select “Multi-signature wallet” -> cosigners = 4, signatures = 3)

Step 4/11

Select “I already have a seed” option. Click “Next”

Step 5/11

Type in your seed. Click “Next”

Step 6/11

On “Master Public Key” screen click “Next” again.

Step 7/11

Select “Enter cosigner key” option. Click “Next”

Step 8/11

Enter second cosigner public key. Click “Next”

Step 9/11

Repeat steps 7 and 8 for other 2 cosigners.

Step 10/11

Choose strong password to encrypt your wallet. Click “Next”

Step 11/11

Now you have Multisig Wallet setup and ready.
