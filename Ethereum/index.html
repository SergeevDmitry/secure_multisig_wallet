Step 1/9

Download MyEtherWallet source code (you need etherwallet-v*.*.*.zip file).

Step 2/9

Disable internet connection.

Step 3/9

Extract the downloaded archive, go to the new folder and double-click on index.html file.

Step 4/9

It will open “Create New Wallet” page in your browser.

Step 5/9

Enter new strong password and press “Create New Wallet” button.

Step 6/9

Download and save keystore file in a safe place. Click “I understand. Continue.”

Step 7/9

Save your private key in a safe place. Click “Save Your Address.”

Step 8/9

Choose “Keystore / JSON File” or “Private Key” option, follow instructions and unlock your wallet.

Step 9/9

Save somewhere your public address.
